  Japans many tourist attractions

Japan has many attractions around the country that many  travel hundreds of miles to see due to the spectacle and allure of these sites. Our beautiful attractions range from the tallest of mountain to some of the highest towers ever made by man. We hope you enjoy this look into our many tourist attractions:


Mount Fuji

Mount Fuji, the famous mountain that gained fame due to its alluring heights, measuring at up to 12,388,making it a major hiker destination. It also sports picturesque views and beautiful cliffside. Our visitors love to explore and see our cities from a new point of view. It is best to view the mountain when our cherry blossom trees are in season due to the many trees in the area.
